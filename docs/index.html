J.R.R. Tolkien was a legendary writer and philologist, best known for his high fantasy works *The Hobbit* and *The Lord of the Rings*.

Why Tolkien is Great

  • He created entire languages (like Sindarin and Quenya)
  • He built an incredibly deep mythology (The Silmarillion)
  • He influenced almost all modern fantasy literature

Some of his most famous quotes:

"Not all those who wander are lost."
"I don't know half of you half as well as I should like; and I like less than half of you half as well as you deserve."
